PowerPoint Presentation Of Lessons For Grade 9 (Quarter 3)

The purpose of this chapter is to lessen these challenges that some educators faced through in this new generations by providing a website that contains the PowerPoint presentation of the grade 10 students in third quarter modules. Teachers and even learners can download the PowerPoint presentation on these website but it does not contains activity because students also have access to the website, there is a 2 or more blank slide on the PPT so when the teacher already downloaded the slides, they can enter an activity, quiz, and even the answer key.
